Who I Am

I am a corporate finance expert turned lifestyle architect. My journey is defined by the belief that true wealth is a holistic pursuit—encompassing financial freedom, physical vitality, and personal elegance.

For over a decade, I navigated the high-pressure world of investment banking. I learned the language of money, strategy, and risk. But I also discovered that success without self-expression is hollow.

Today, I bridge these worlds. I teach you how to command a room with your presence and your P&L.

My Transformation Journey

apartment
2012 - 2020

Foundation in Finance

I built my foundation in finance across corporate, government, and consulting roles. I mastered systems, strategy, and complex problem-solving, earning credibility through discipline and excellence. Externally, I was successful; internally, I was searching for deeper meaning. I began to realise that true wealth is not just financial — it is also physical, emotional, and personal.

self_improvement
2021 - 2024

Season of Awakening

I entered a season of awakening. I invested in my wellbeing, my confidence, and my self-expression through fitness, personal development, and fashion. I started showing up more intentionally — not just as a finance professional, but as a woman stepping into her power. This period shaped the philosophy behind VM Heiress: that confidence is built through clarity, discipline, and self-belief.

psychology
August 2024

The Turning Point (Kaizen)

My journey reached a turning point when I joined the 100-Day Challenge (Kaizen). This experience transformed me from the inside out. I developed strength, resilience, and consistency — not only in my body, but in my mindset and leadership. Kaizen helped me reconnect with my potential and reminded me that transformation is a continuous evolution.

2025 - 2026

Rebirth & Emergence

I am in my season of rebirth and emergence. Through my modeling journey with Country Girl Management, I am embracing presence, poise, and creative expression. At the same time, I am building VM Heiress and nurturing VM Elite Circle — a community for women and men who aspire to grow in:

  • Finance — smart strategy, wealth mindset, and empowerment
  • Fitness — discipline, strength, and self-love
  • Fashion & Personal Brand — confidence, style, and self-expression

VM Heiress is more than a brand — it is a movement for those who want to become the most powerful, polished, and purposeful version of themselves.

My journey proves that transformation is possible.

Your journey begins with belief.
